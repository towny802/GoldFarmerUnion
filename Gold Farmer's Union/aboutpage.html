Welcome

Welcome to the Gold Farmer's Union! This is a website for entrepreneurial gold farmer's to sell digital property in exchange for real money. We at the Union named ourselves on the idea that like unionized laborers, gold farmers ought to have representation and security. We hope that you find this website to offer both, and would like you contact us to let us know how we can do better.

Getting Started

We're a pretty simple website, so getting started with the Gold Farmer's Union should be easy! Just go to 'Account,' fill out a registration, and enjoy our services.

Buying

To buy with the Gold Farmers' Union, go to the 'Buy' tab, and fill in search criteria for what you're looking for. Use the tags to help narrow your search results. The Gold Farmers' Union can in no way ensure you will get the items you purchase, so make sure to only buy from vendors you know and trust!

Selling

Selling items on our market is quick and easy. Fill in the form on the selling page, and a listing for that item will be made available to the public. When someone purchases your item, you wil receive an notification. Be sure to check your email often! Once the sale is made, get in contact with your customer, and give him what he ordered.
